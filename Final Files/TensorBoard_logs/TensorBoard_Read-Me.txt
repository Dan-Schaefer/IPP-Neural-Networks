In order to view TensorBoard logs:

(At least on Windows 10)

1.) Launch Anaconda Prompt
2.) Type:    tensorboard --logdir="C:\tmp\keras_logs"
3.) Open Browser (Google Chrome works, I can't make guarantees about others)
4.) Type:    localhost:6006 (or whatever the console prompts you to do)
